Webquest

The question

In this lesson we concentrated on Jefferson and the writing of the Declaration of Independence, before beginning, think about what else you know of Thomas Jefferson?

  • What would you consider his most important achievement?
  • What important document did Thomas Jefferson write?
  • What university did he start?
  • What was the name of his plantation?
  • After attending the College of William and Mary, what did Jefferson do?
